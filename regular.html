Regular is a project developed during my 3rd year of bachelor's degree, the subject was to simply create a site, create content and bring traffic. My choice fell on a platform highlighting typographies made by independent designers mainly, to improve designer's research that can be really time-consuming.

ROLES Art direction Webdesign Web Dev.
LINKS Regular
No transition between pages, no fancy animations on hover. The point of this website was to make it as lightweight as possible to keep the brutalist/minimalistic feeling.
